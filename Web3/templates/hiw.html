HOW IT WORKS

Select Wallet

  1. Select Web3 wallet, or sign up for TrueSPAC Web3 Wallet.
  2. Buy, and or Swap Crypto for your desired RDUC.

Crypto Debit Card

  1. Sign up for virtual Crypto debit card for free, or order your physical card of choice.
  2. Follow card instructions, and top up your card with RDUC Crypto (Earn rewards using RDUC tokens) or select other listed Crypto.

Send & Receive Crypto

  1. Send Web3 funds/Crypto to wallet address of choice, by selecting recipient wallet address from your contact list, or use the recipient Web3 personal domain name as a wallet address. Ex joepiper.jamaica-wi & that's it.
  2. After receiving Web3 funds in your wallet, top up your virtual or physical Crypto debit card, and use it like cash, anywhere Visa or MasterCard is accepted.
