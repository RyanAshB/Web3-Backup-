What is a Web3 personal domain name?

Web3 personal domain name, is a secondary domain name, linked to a top level domain name (TLD).

Example: Joepiper.jamaica-wi is a secondary domain name, with .jamaica-wi being the TLD.

How does a Web3 personal domain name work with Remitter?

A Web3 personal domain name, once integrated and mapped to a wallet address, can take the place of the long string of characters typically associated with a Crypto wallet address.

Example: 1A1zP1eP5QGefi2DMPTfTL5SLmv7DivfNa is mapped, and replaced with joepiper.jamaica.wi
